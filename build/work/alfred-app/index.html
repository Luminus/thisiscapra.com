Alfred App

Ollie was approached by talented developer and marketing team Andrew and Vero Pepperell to create the original design and branding for a quicklauncher that simplified and sped up your workflow. Its launch went viral (with web designers and other mac users who love all things beautiful) and has been successful ever since. Alfred App is your computer’s butler: it is a highly rated and popular quick launcher for OSX that has been used by tens of thousands of Mac users worldwide.

  • Branding & logo design

  • Marketing materials
  • Iconography

Capra continues to design for Alfred, working on everything from OSX icon design and branding through to marketing materials and web presence. Over the 5 years since its launch, the product has evolved many new features and expanded to users all over the world.
