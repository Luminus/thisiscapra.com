Alton Convent is a highly rated public school located in the picturesque village of Alton, UK. Before, they had an outdated drupal system that was very difficult to update and didn't have the flexibility the school needed to move forward. They came to us with a simple brief: ‘Make it beautiful, easy to use and stand out from the crowd’. We built them a lovely design based around full screen photography and a fully responsive front end build to work across all devices. The new site is built on a customised wordpress install with extended scalability.

  • Web design
  • Branding
  • Project management
  • Wordpress CMS
  • Photo Gallery
  • Responsive build
  • Marketing materials

The Convent’s design has become a benchmark for other premium schools and has received compliments from both staff and parents. They have made their website a cornerstone to their communication with parents, with up-to-date mobile technology, a fully featued events calendar, social media integration, newsletters as well as integration with existing school systems.
