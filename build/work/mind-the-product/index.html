MindtheProduct is a very popular meetup group for product managers, and in 2012 they launched their very first conference in London, UK. Kat was approached to come up with a simple, elegant design. The first year’s website was a simple one-page design, based on their brand’s colours and logo, where the circles moved as you scrolled.

  • Web design
  • Responsive build
  • HTML/CSS integration
  • Wordpress Multi-Site

Because we were both so pleased with the first year’s efforts, we knew we had to come up with something fun and different for the second year. So, 2013 website features a video background from last year’s conference. This website was designed to inspire product managers to make great, innovative products.

Then in 2014, the founders decided to go for simple and practical yet elegant. We created a website that could be modified through Wordpress, and could be reused for future years. The build needed to be flexible for 2015 to adapt to multiple locations (San Francisco and London this year!), pre-conference (no speakers, just teasers), full conference (speakers, tickets for sale) and post conference (wrap up, thank you and archiving). We continue to work with Martin and the rest of the MindTheProduct team to keep their website fresh!
